Monero community voters used ranked-choice voting to elect one committee member for the MAGIC Monero Fund. The winner will join two existing committee members. The MAGIC Monero Fund supports projects in the Monero ecosystem.

## Results of the Election

MAGIC Grants is pleased to announce the winner of the election, [Zach](https://github.com/MAGICGrants/Monero-Fund-Elections/issues/17) from [Unredacted](https://unredacted.org/about/us/).

His term will begin on January 31, 2026.
