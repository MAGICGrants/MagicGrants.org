The MAGIC Privacy Guides Fund and the MAGIC Monero Fund recently launched their own merchandise shops. Proceeds from these shops support their respective fund and associated charitable activities.

Merchandise can be shipped to many jurisdictions. Whether you are shopping for clothing, dinnerware, or accessories, these items make great gifts this holiday season and support a good cause.

[Privacy Guides Shop](https://shop.privacyguides.org){: .btn-primary}   [Monero Fund Shop](https://shop.monerofund.org){: .btn-primary}

Currently, cryptocurrency payments are only available by request.

Thank you for supporting the MAGIC Privacy Guides Fund and the MAGIC Monero Fund this holiday season!
